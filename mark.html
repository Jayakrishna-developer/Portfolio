    • Hi, It's Mark Zuckerberg

    I am the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Meta Platforms, formerly known as Facebook. I launched Facebook from my dormitory room on February 4, 2004, with my college roommates. Over the years, Facebook has grown to become one of the largest social media platforms in the world, connecting billions of people globally. I am passionate about technology and innovation, continuously working towards creating tools that help people stay connected and share their experiences.

    Follow My Journey

    PROJECTS

    Facebook (Meta Platforms)

    I co-founded Facebook, which revolutionized the way people connect and share online. From its inception in my dorm room to becoming a global platform, Facebook has been at the forefront of social networking innovation.

    Oculus VR

    Acquiring Oculus VR was a significant step in our journey to advance virtual reality. Through Oculus, we are pioneering immersive experiences and pushing the boundaries of what is possible in digital interaction.

    WhatsApp and Messenger

    Integrating WhatsApp and Messenger into our ecosystem has transformed how people communicate globally. These platforms are not only convenient but also prioritize security and privacy for billions of users.

    Internet.org

    Through Internet.org, I have worked towards providing internet access to underserved regions. This initiative aims to bridge the digital divide and empower communities with information and connectivity.

    Digital Economy and Cryptocurrency

    With the Libra project, rebranded as Diem, I explored the potential of blockchain technology to create a global digital currency. This initiative seeks to provide financial inclusion and stability to the global economy.
